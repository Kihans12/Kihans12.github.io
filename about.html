    What is a Kihans Shop ?

    Kihans Shop is an online shop whose scope is only owned by Kihans Shop, which was created to promote goods sold at Kihans Shop, so that it is exposed to regions within the country and abroad.
    KihansShop only sells a few types of goods, namely clothes, trousers, shoes and accessories. Hopefully in the future there will be more items sold at the Kihans Shop. and hopefully visitors to this online shop will be comfortable using it. We as developers of this online shop hope for suggestions from users, so that this shop can be even better and can be used with what is shown.
    Thank you all and thank you for your support...

    What is the purpose of creating this online shop ?
